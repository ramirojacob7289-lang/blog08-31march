Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Insight Media Group LLC ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, login status, and browsing behavior.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (which are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., authentication, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies to verify user identity
  • Security cookies to prevent fraudulent use
  • Session management cookies
  • Cookie consent preferences

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

We use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information about:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Traffic sources and referral websites
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • User engagement and bounce rates

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your choices and preferences:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Font size and display settings
  • Previously entered form data
  • Content personalization preferences

3.4 Advertising and Marketing Cookies

We and our advertising partners use cookies to:

  • Display relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see an advertisement
  • Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
  • Retarget visitors who have previously interacted with our content

Third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdSense, Outbrain, and Facebook) may also place cookies on your device when you visit our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses services from third-party providers who may set their own cookies:

4.1 Analytics Providers

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis and user behavior tracking
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Advertising Networks

  • Google AdSense: Contextual and display advertising
  • Outbrain: Content recommendation platform
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising and retargeting

4.3 Social Media Platforms

  • Social media sharing buttons may set cookies when you interact with them
  • These cookies are controlled by the respective social media platforms

5. How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookie duration varies by type:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period (typically 30 days to 2 years)
  • Third-Party Cookies: Controlled by external providers, with varying expiration dates

6. Managing Cookie Preferences

6.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice will be remembered for future visits.

6.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept cookies only from specific websites
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set your browser to notify you when cookies are sent

Please note that blocking essential cookies may prevent certain features of our website from functioning properly.

6.3 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

6.4 Opt-Out Tools

You can opt-out of certain third-party cookies using these tools:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Advertising: Network Advertising Initiative Opt-out
  • European Users: Your Online Choices

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. Our website does not currently respond to DNT signals, but we provide cookie management options as described above.

8. Mobile Device Identifiers

When you access our website via a mobile device, we may collect device identifiers and use similar technologies to cookies. You can manage these through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

9. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our practices. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
